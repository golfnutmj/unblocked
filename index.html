Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Is Unblocked free?

Yes ‚Äî completely free during beta. We're focused on building the best experience possible based on your feedback. Pricing for the full release will be announced later.

How does data sync work?

Your boards are stored in Firebase and sync automatically in real time. Open Unblocked on your laptop, make a change, and see it update on your phone instantly.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Core features work offline via local storage. When you reconnect, your changes sync back to Firebase automatically.

Is my data secure?

During beta, Firebase runs in test mode for easier access. Full authentication, private boards, and encrypted storage are coming in v1.0. Avoid storing sensitive information for now.

Can I collaborate with others?

Collaboration is in development. Currently, anyone with a board URL can view and edit cards ‚Äî great for testing, with proper access controls coming soon.

How do I install it on iPhone or iPad?

Open this page in Safari, tap the Share icon, then choose "Add to Home Screen." It will look and feel like a native app.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. Hit the Export button on any board to download your cards as JSON. You can also import boards to restore or share them.
