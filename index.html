Frequently Asked Questions

Is Unblocked really free?

Yes! During beta testing, Unblocked is completely free. We're gathering feedback and improving the product. We may introduce paid plans in the future, but beta users will receive special benefits.

Do I need to create an account?

Not during beta! Your board is stored in the cloud and accessible via URL. Bookmark it or add to your home screen to access anytime. (Authentication coming in future release.)

How do I access my board on multiple devices?

Simple! Just bookmark the URL (golfnutmj.github.io/unblocked/board.html) on each device. Your boards sync automatically via Firebase. For mobile, tap "Add to Home Screen" in Safari for app-like experience.

Is my data safe?

Your boards are stored in Google Firebase, a secure cloud platform. During beta, boards are publicly accessible if someone knows your URL. Full authentication and private boards coming soon!

What happens if I close my browser?

All your data is saved in the cloud. Nothing is lost when you close your browser. Just return to the same URL and everything will be there.

Can I share my board with my team?

Yes! Just share the URL. Everyone who opens it will see the same board with real-time updates. Perfect for small teams. (Advanced permissions coming later.)

Does it work offline?

Limited offline support currently. You can view your board offline, but changes require internet connection to sync. Better offline support coming in future updates.

How do I report bugs or request features?

We'd love your feedback! During beta, please email bugs and suggestions to: feedback@unblocked.app
